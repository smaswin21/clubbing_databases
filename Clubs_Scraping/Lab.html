Lab theClub

  • lab_the_club_2.jpg
  • lab_theclub.jpg
  • lab_the_club.jpg

Description

Information

Located on the first floor of Chamartín Station - Clara Campoamor, this legendary establishment, home to the Macumba  macro-disco, which then changed its name to Space of Sound, has  been totally refurbished and adapted to suit today’s needs, where the user’s experience will reach levels of fun that have never been seen before, making  each night unique and unrepeatable. 

Lab theClub is equipped with d&b audiotechnik, a modern sound system that ensures the highest sound quality and efficiency, favouring the immersive experience that the club proposes for its nights. In its bid for connectivity and the digitalization of all its services, it also allows any product to be ordered directly from the menu from any of the different spaces into which the establishment is divided thanks to the installation of tablet devices all over.

LAB theClub offers a programme of leading international DJs, including the resident DJs,   Chus, Sparrow & Barbossa, La Santa, Macarena Hoffman, Pomboklap or Chris Figueroa, among others.

Thursday nights, known as BeRenatta, will include the presence of international DJs specialised in tech-house.

On Fridays, it is time for Bandidos, where you will be able to escape from reality by entering into the sound world with the most mainstream session.

Oh Madre! is every Saturday with a surprising music and stage proposal, where house, afro-house and latin-house sounds come together.

The Crow Techno Club techno sessions will be programmed on specific dates. In these sessions, you will be able to enjoy the best artists within the darkest spectrum of electronic music.

In addition to music, patrons will be able to watch surprising and provocative shows and even a gastronomic drunch  created expressly for this new club concept.
