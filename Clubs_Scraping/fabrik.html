Fabrik

  • fab_1429270232.61.jpg
  • fab2_1429270235.35.jpg
  • fab3_1429270237.975.jpg

Description

Information

This club, opened in 2003, is considered one of the best clubs in the world. The room has magnificent facilities to enjoy a fun night out such as supervised parking, main floor with multiple VIP areas, a terrace with dance area and 7 bars spread throughout the premises.

But if Fabrik stands out for anything, it is for its programme of events organised by some of the best national promoters such as Goa or Supermartxe, and with performances by the best national and international artists such as Carl Cox, Sven Vath, Luciano, Villalobos or Marco Carola.

You can get here by car or by public transport, but Fabrik also has a shuttle bus service that runs continuously when the club is open.

  • - Option 1: Departure from the Temple of Debod (Calle del professor Martn Almagro Basch, 6). €3 each way (final stop at Prncipe Po station).
  • - Option 2: Departure from Calle Luis Sauquillo, 67, in Fuenlabrada. €2 each way.
